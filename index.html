How It Works

Simple Steps to Convert

Follow this easy guide to get your conversion results in seconds.

1

Set Your Rate

Enter your custom rate in the 'Your Rate' field. If you leave it blank, the live official rate will be used automatically.
2

Choose Direction

Click the toggle buttons to select whether you are converting from MMK to THB, or from THB to MMK.
3

Enter Amount

Type the amount of money you wish to convert into the 'Amount to Convert' field.

Get Instant Result

The converted amount appears instantly in the result box. Use the 'Copy' button to save the details.
